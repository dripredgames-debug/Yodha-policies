About the Developer

Hi, I’m Alfred, the creator of Drip Red Games. As an introvert, I’ve often found social gatherings challenging, feeling out of place in a crowd. This inspired me to become an indie game developer, driven by a passion to design innovative board games that offer a fresh, engaging experience. My goal is to craft games that bring people together, spark joy, and provide an escape from awkward social moments, allowing players to connect through unique and creative gameplay. If you have feedback or ideas, reach out at dripredgames@gmail.com. Let’s create something extraordinary together!
